Resources

The mission of the Office of Digital Learning is to accelerate the development of degree, advanced certificate, and non-credit online programs at Teachers College. We recognize that e-learning has become an important part of education for K-12, Higher Education, and lifelong learning and, therefore, work to support faculty in expanding their teaching toolkits with new digital teaching strategies and techniques backed by research and informed by practice. Through our efforts we aim to expand the reach of Teachers College beyond its walls.

Preparing for Online Learning at TC

This online orientation is part of a pilot initiative that aims to help online learners at Teachers College prepare for their online learning experiences. This course supplements the orientation information online learners will receive on their course site. While some content may be repeated, learners will find additional tips and practice activities in this course that may be helpful to them.

ODL Faculty Orientation for Online Development

This course is designed to help faculty as they begin thinking about their programs, provide documents and resources to guide them throughout the process, and showcase exemplary e-learning assets that we can use as models as we design and develop online programming.
