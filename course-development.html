Phases of Online Course Development

INQUIRY

Faculty meet with Steve and Rochelle to explore program concept and determine viability

PRE APPROVAL

Faculty submit course for department and state approval

ADMISSIONS, MARKETING, & OUTREACH

Faculty meet wiht ODL, Marketing team, and Admissions to set application standards and provide information for marketing and communications.

REVIEW

Although not part of the course development process, this phase is part of the lifecycle of the entire online development and delivery process

DESIGN

Faculty meet with ODL to plan the desired outcomes, structure, logistics, and development timeline for the course

DELIVERY

The content of the course is delivered to students

DEVELOPMENT

ODL helps faculty identify technology and media that will be used in the course and designs educational media as needed
